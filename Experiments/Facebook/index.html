Facebook Phone Search

During 2019 in the wake of a scandal caused by their careless distribution of user data, Facebook removed the ability to search for Facebook profiles by phone number.


Humanitarian groups, investigators and reporters, and the Open Source Intelligence community was suddenly without one of the strongest methods of identifying profiles on the most popular social media platform.


While I can't make Facebook reverse its decision, I can undermine it. Using leaked data from 2019, I've compiled a database of over 30-million profiles and their associated U.S. phone numbers, and made them searchable at this address.


Enter a US phone number (with or without a leading 1), hit enter or the search button, and be automatically redirected to the associated profile. Good luck on your investigations!
